Services

Car Detailing

The process of imrpoving the condition
of a vehicle's interior and exterior appearance.

Water Service

We hand prep your vehicle with a high pressure
gun and a soft foam brush to clean all the
areas that are difficult for the equipment
package to reach.

Ceramic Coating

A semi-permanent or permanent of coating that
is applied to the exterior of your vehicle to protect
against external paint damage.

Teflon Coating

A synthetic sealant that binds to your car's
paint and forms a layer over it to provide
protection from minor scratches.

De-carbonize

A mechanical or chemical process in which carbon
deposits on the pistons and the cylinder
head are removed to ensure smooth and better
functioning of the overall engine.

Sterillize

Sterillize your car interior and exterior
and keep you and your car free from germs

Under Coating

A protective layer that is sprayed all
over the undercarriage of your vehicle
